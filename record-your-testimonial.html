Hello and welcome to the Hold Me Caregiver Testimonial section, our global outreach campaign!

Hold Me is a fictional feature film about a caregiver who accompanies people through doctor assisted dying.

The Caregiver Testimonial section is a collection of real life caregiver experiences from professionals all over the worls.

We want the film Hold Me to draw attention to the real life wisdom and experience of caregivers who work closely with patients at the end of life.

Through this, we hope to create a place of reflection and catharsis for those searching for insigh into grief, loss and acceptance. Available online, for free, catalogued by occupation and country.

The Hold Me Caregiver Testimonial section will begin releasing audio recordings after 50 have been collected.

Thank you for contributing what you have to say!

Take Part

Here are instructions on how you can take part in the Caregiver Outreach Campaign by leaving a recording of your caregiver testimonial.

How to record your testimonial

We recomend every answer is about 2 minutes in length.

Press and replay your message so you know your microphone is working properly.

Record your message. Press and then .

You can press to listen to your message as many times as you like and repeat steps 1 and 2 until you are happy.

Fill in your contact details is optional but recommended so we can contact you. Then press and your message will be delivered to our inbox.

Record

Please begin recording by stating:

Your first name (if you wish).

Your occupation working closely with the dying.

The country in which this work was done.

Questions:

1. What is a valuable story or account you can share about someone you have accompanied?

2. What advice would you offer people struggling with expressing or coping with their feelings?

3. How do you manage the emotional toll that working with the dying can bring?

4. What do you feel people want from a caregiver at the end of life?

5. What has working with the dying taught you about life?

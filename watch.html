Hold Me is the story of Hannah, a woman whose job it is to hold and console people who are being voluntarily euthanized.

Filmed in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada and New York, NY, Hold Me is Teace Snyder and Aksel Stasny's first feature film together. Starring Hannah Fierman and Laura Kenny as mother and daughter, the film challenges us to glimpse the final moments of life in the context of a loving goodbye–to consider the struggles and sacrifices of those who work in palliative care–and to find the courage to talk about it.
